# Messaging System

Messaging is a separate product layer.

## Seniors and Families

Core promise:

Find care, support, events, and trusted local help without pressure.

Messaging rules:

- Lead with dignity, calm, and clarity.
- Avoid fear-based crisis copy.
- Emphasize free direct contact.
- Explain that sponsored placements are labeled.
- Make the app feel useful before and after a care decision.

Primary claims:

- Complete local options, not only paid partners.
- Free direct contact with providers.
- Community events and resources near you.
- Save, compare, and share care options.
- Transparent source and listing status.

## Operators and Providers

Core promise:

Your listing is free. Your local growth engine is paid.

Messaging rules:

- Lead with owned local demand, not leads.
- Contrast with referral-fee models without sounding bitter.
- Explain that paid growth is campaigns, events, reviews, ads, and automation.
- Make $100/month-style plans feel like managed growth, not another dashboard.

Primary claims:

- No move-in referral commissions.
- Free listing and free direct contact.
- Claim, verify, and enrich your profile.
- Promote events and community education.
- Run AI SEO, social, review, chat, and voice campaigns.
- Buy clearly labeled local placements.

## Shared Trust Rules

- Do not blur sponsored and organic content.
- Do not imply medical, legal, financial, or placement advice without review.
- Do not claim "best" or "safest" without verified evidence.
- Do not publish AI content under a human byline without approval.
- Do not republish full third-party articles without license.
